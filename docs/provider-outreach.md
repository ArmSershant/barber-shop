# Provider Outreach — talking to barbers & shop owners

Everything you need to walk into a shop (or message an owner) and get them to sign
up at **barber-shop.am**. Messages are ready to copy; Armenian is primary
(Yerevan audience), with Russian and English variants.

> Honest framing for today: listing and taking bookings is **free right now** —
> there is no commission or fee yet. Say "free to join and take bookings," not
> "free forever." (A commission model is planned but not live.)

---

## 1. One-line pitch

barber-shop.am is a free online booking page for Yerevan barbers and shops:
clients find you, book a time 24/7, and keep coming back — you control everything.

## 2. Why a barber/shop should join (benefits that are LIVE)

- **Free to list and take bookings.** No fee today.
- **Your own page** with photos, portfolio, services, prices and working hours —
  no website needed, we build it.
- **Online booking 24/7.** Clients pick a service and a free time; you either
  auto-confirm or approve each request.
- **Full schedule control** — working hours, breaks, time off, and per-service
  duration/price.
- **Loyalty points you control** — set how many points per 100 ֏ and what a point
  is worth. Keeps clients booking *through you*, not drifting away.
- **Promotions & first-visit discounts** — run a time-limited % off, or give new
  clients a discount, shown right on the site.
- **Waitlist** — when someone cancels, waiting clients are notified automatically,
  so fewer empty chairs.
- **Reviews you can reply to** — build reputation, answer feedback.
- **Analytics** — revenue, bookings, repeat clients, no-show rate.
- **Fewer no-shows** — automatic booking reminders.
- **Trilingual** (Armenian / English / Russian) and a **custom page link**
  (e.g. barber-shop.am/barbers/your-name).

## 4. Objection handling (FAQ)

- **"Is it really free?"** Yes — today there's no fee or commission to list and
  take bookings.
- **"I don't have a website / I'm not technical."** You don't need one. We create
  your page; you just add photos, services and your hours. It takes minutes.
- **"I already have my own clients."** Good — the loyalty points and your own
  booking link keep them booking through the site, and reminders cut no-shows.
- **"What if I get double-booked?"** Impossible — the system blocks overlapping
  bookings and respects your breaks and time off.
- **"Can I approve who books?"** Yes — turn on "requires approval" and accept or
  reject each request. Or auto-confirm.
- **"Can I set my own prices / discounts?"** Fully. Prices per service, promo %,
  first-visit discount and loyalty rate are all yours to set.

## 5. Sign them up on the spot

1. Go to **barber-shop.am/register**.
2. Choose role: **Barber** (independent) or **Shop owner**.
3. Fill name + email + password; verify the email.
4. In the dashboard: add photo/cover, services + prices, working hours.
5. (Optional but great) turn on loyalty points, a first-visit discount, and the
   waitlist — all in the profile settings.
6. Share their new link: barber-shop.am/barbers/<their-name> (they can edit it).
